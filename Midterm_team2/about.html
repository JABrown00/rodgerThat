About


Welcome to the World Wide Cafe, designed and created by MaKenna Schrader and Justin Brown; these two are distinguished members of Team 2 in CS3500. Here, we offer a variety of food from different regions of the globe. We have food from all the continents barring Antarctica and from each of those continents, we have foods from different countries and cultures. We pride ourselves based on our different types of food, ranging from breakfast to dinner options. Our customer top picks are the Greek Gyros and the different European crepes available.


Awards


Worldwide Cafe has won more than 50 Grand Restaurant Awards; it gets awarded to restaurants countrywide. We have also been published in multiple newspapers because of our unique, worldly atmosphere and our genuine hospitality.
